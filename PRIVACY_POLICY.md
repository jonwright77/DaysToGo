## Information We Collect

### 1. Location Data

**What we collect:** Your device's precise geographic location coordinates, including latitude, longitude, timestamp, and accuracy measurements.

**How we collect it:** The app uses background location services to continuously track your location throughout the day.

**Why we collect it:** To build a location history so you can see where you were on reflection dates when viewing reminders. This helps provide context about your past activities.

**How long we keep it:** Location data is stored locally on your device and via your private iCloud account for 90 days. Data older than 90 days is automatically deleted.

**Frequency:** The app records your location approximately every 20 meters of movement and always records your first location each day.

### 2. Photos

**What we collect:** Access to photos from your photo library based on the dates they were taken.

**How we collect it:** The app reads your photo library when you view a reminder to display photos from the corresponding reflection date.

**Why we collect it:** To show you photos from your past that correspond to the reflection dates of your reminders, helping you remember what you were doing.

**How we use it:** Photos are displayed within the app only. We do not upload, copy, or modify your photos.

### 3. Calendar Events

**What we collect:** Read-only access to events from calendars you select within the app.

**How we collect it:** The app reads calendar events when you view a reminder to display events from the corresponding reflection date.

**Why we collect it:** To show you what calendar events you had on reflection dates, providing context about your past schedule.

**How we use it:** Calendar data is read-only and displayed within the app. We do not create, modify, or delete calendar events.

### 4. Reminder Data

**What we collect:**
- Reminder titles and dates
- Optional descriptions
- Selected background colors
- Creation and modification timestamps

**How we collect it:** You provide this information when creating or editing reminders.

**Why we collect it:** This is the core functionality of the app - to track your countdowns and reminders.

**How long we keep it:** Indefinitely, until you delete a reminder or uninstall the app.

### 5. User Profile Information

**What we collect:**
- First name (required during onboarding)
- Surname (optional)
- Country (selected during onboarding)

**How we collect it:** You provide this information during the onboarding process.

**Why we collect it:** To personalize your experience with greetings and profile display.

**How we use it:** Displayed in the Settings menu and used for personalized greetings throughout the app.

### 6. Display Preferences

**What we collect:** Your preferences for which data types to display (Photos, Calendar Events, Historical Events, Location).

**How we collect it:** You configure these settings in the Display Options menu.

**Why we collect it:** To customize which information appears in your reminder details based on your preferences.

## How We Store Your Data

### Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:
- Location history (last 90 days)
- User preferences and settings
- Display preferences
- Calendar preferences

### iCloud Storage (Private)

The following data is synced to your private iCloud account:
- Reminders (titles, dates, descriptions, colors)
- User profile information (name, country)
- Location history (last 90 days)

**Important:** All data synced to iCloud is stored in your **private** iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud data. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Apple's CloudKit encryption.

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties.**

- Location data: Stored only on your device and your private iCloud account
- Photos: Only read from your library, never copied or uploaded
- Calendar events: Only read from your device, never copied or uploaded
- Reminders: Stored only in your private iCloud account
- Profile information: Stored only on your device and your private iCloud account

## Third-Party Services

### Apple CloudKit

We use Apple's CloudKit service to sync your data across your devices. CloudKit stores data in your private iCloud account. Apple's privacy policy applies to this service: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

### Wikipedia API

We use Wikipedia's free public API to fetch historical events for the "On This Day" feature. This service does not require authentication and does not track users. Wikipedia's privacy policy: https://foundation.wikimedia.org/wiki/Privacy_policy

**No analytics or tracking services:** We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any other third-party analytics or tracking services.

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control

You have full control over your data:

- **View your data:** All your data is accessible within the app
- **Delete reminders:** Swipe to delete any reminder at any time
- **Delete location history:** Automatically deleted after 90 days, or uninstall the app
- **Disable features:** Toggle off Photos, Calendar, Historical Events, or Location in Display Options
- **Revoke permissions:** Disable location, photo, or calendar access in iOS Settings at any time
- **Delete all data:** Uninstalling the app deletes all local data. iCloud data can be removed by disabling iCloud for the app in iOS Settings

### Location Privacy

- **Background location is optional:** The app functions without location tracking
- **Always permission:** We request "Always" permission to build location history in the background
- **Disable anytime:** You can disable location access in iOS Settings → Days To Go → Location → Never
- **90-day limit:** Location data older than 90 days is automatically deleted
- **No sharing:** Your location is never shared with third parties or uploaded to our servers

### Photo and Calendar Privacy

- **Read-only access:** We only read your photos and calendar events, never modify or delete them
- **On-demand access:** Photos and calendar data are only accessed when you view a reminder
- **No uploads:** Photos and calendar events stay on your device and are never uploaded
- **Disable anytime:** Revoke access in iOS Settings → Days To Go → Photos/Calendars

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption in transit:** All CloudKit sync uses HTTPS encryption
- **Encryption at rest:** iCloud data is encrypted using Apple's CloudKit encryption
- **Local storage:** Data is protected by your device's security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
- **No server storage:** We do not operate our own servers or store your data outside your device and iCloud account

## Children's Privacy

Days To Go is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
